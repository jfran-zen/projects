Our Services:

Flowers INK provides the following services, but where we really shine is individualizing your vision for your wedding or event. Feel free to schedule a free consultation to discuss options.

Wedding party floral arrangements:

This includes bridal bouquets, bridesmaid bouquets, corsages for the wedding party, mother of the bride, and mother of the groom, flower girl corsages and flower girl crowns. We customize each option depending on your style.

Ceremony floral arrangements:

This includes floral design for altars, arches, aisle design including chairs, and entrance to the ceremony. We are open to unique ideas and love to help realize your vision. We have done work in all kinds of venues including indoor, outdoor, farmhouse, mountain, etc!

Reception design floral arrangements:

This can include head table or sweetheart table floral arrangements, decorations for the dining tables, cake tables, welcome sign and welcome table, wedding cake including toppers, adding flowers to the cake itself, and cake table floral arrangements, and beverage table. This is where we find we can often be the most creative!

Other:

Flower crowns, dog collars, or anything you can imagine! We love new ideas and coming up with personalized touches that are meaningful to you.

Flower Fact: Do you know that Dandelions are also a source of vitamins? Let me tell you that the leaves and flowers of the dandelion plant are rich in Vitamin A, C, K, Calcium, Iron and Potassium. In addition, they also contain small amounts of Vitamin E, B, and Folate.
