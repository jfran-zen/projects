Frequently asked questions

  • How far in advance is booking required? Four months is good for planning purposes, but Flowers INK can work with all kinds of timelines, feel free to ask!
  • Does Flowers INK provide mock ups or sample arrangements? Yes, we do! We love to show off our talents for your specific needs.
  • Does Flowers INK provide consultations? The reason we started this business is because we enjoy helping people make their day beautiful with floral arrangements. We like to get to know you face to face if possible. It is possible to work over the phone, but find we are able to see your personality and vision better with an in-person meeting. A consultation is always free. We like to keep things easy and casual, meeting over a cup of coffee is preferred!
  • Does Flowers INK charge a delivery or destination fee? Yes, it will be included in the package price after the initial consultation.
  • What is Flowers INK’s service area? Anywhere! We are primarily located in Northern California but are willing to discuss travel options. We love to travel and enjoy new locations and experiences.
  • What should I expect for investment? Every package is individualized. We work with your design and your budget to provide you with the best options for your event.
  • What kinds of services do we provide? We primarily do floral design for weddings, including bouquets and table decorations. We specialize in floral design for tables and archways. We even have done dog collars and flower crowns! We have also done birthday parties and formal events.
