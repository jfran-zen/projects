Welcome to Flowers INK!

We are a floral design business by Ingrid McMahon and Kelly Glassburn. We love to individualize floral design for weddings and events and specialize in adding personal touches to your day! It is our passion to help you realize your vision and to make your event special. Please have a look around and contact us for any needs or requests. We would love to collaborate with you to provide your dream design to complement your specific style.

At Flowers INK, we are able to provide wedding and event services, based in the Northern California area and beyond. We love to travel and make each event unique and beautiful with our exquisite flower arrangements. If you have an idea, we can make it happen! Or if you are looking for a certain style, we can come up with design ideas for you. We know that wedding planning can involve a lot, and we want to make it easier on you. We have pictures provided if you want an idea of what we have done for other events. Our goal is for you to be able to have a beautiful event and we work with your ideas and budget.
