About Us

Who We Are:

We are Ingrid and Kelly, owners and designers of FlowersINK. We met while working as educators with special education children. During that time we discovered a shared interest in all things flowers, and the rest is history. Our first jobs were weddings for our daughters, and it was so much fun that a business was born. You’ll find we are passionate about creating a floral experience that reflects you. We enjoy getting to know our clients and making their big day beautiful. As former educators we know the importance of listening so we get the look you want just right. It is important to us to connect with you and your love story. A business that starts by hearing you, by becoming friends by making your wedding flowers unique to you and the story of your love.

About Ingrid:

Ingrid has always had a love for flowers and creative spirit as a former elementary school teacher. She created the flowers and decorations for her daughter’s wedding in 2014 and realized that she had a knack for floral design. She states that she loves the moment when the design comes together.

Favorite flower (of the moment!): anemone

About Kelly:

Kelly has been creating floral arrangements for years. She has been working with Ingrid to provide beautiful floral arrangements and enjoys working with the customer to create their specific vision.

Favorite flower: Classic roses
